Downloading from the Workshop

Please note that downloading from the Steam Workshop is available to Steam customers only. Redeem the Steam activation code you've received with your purchase to use the Steam Workshop.

Enhancing your game with mods from the Steam Workshop is easy in After the Collapse. There are two ways of acquiring mods via the Steam Workshop:

In-game

At After the Collapse's main menu, click "Mods", and then "Mod Browser" under "Steam Workshop". Click on a mod you like, then click "Subscribe". The mod will be downloaded immediately and you'll be prompted to restart your game to apply the mod.

Via the online interface at https://steamcommunity.com/workshop

Open After the Collapse's Steam Workshop, browse for a mod you'd like to install, and click the "Subscribe" button, and the mod will be available the next time you open After the Collapse.
